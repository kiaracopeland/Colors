Artists and Their Creations

Leonardo Di Vinci (before the colorwheel)

Some may not know but, the Mona Lisa (1503) was painted before the color wheel was invented. Look at her. she came out perfectly fine.

Frida Kahlo (after the color wheel)

Now here is Frida Kahlo. Her art comes afer the color wheel was created, although some may point out that her paintings have more of a vibrancy in color. Let us not forget the dark reflections of pain and agony that go hand in hand with Frida's work.
